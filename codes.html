Effects of Enrollment Digitalization on School's Retention

With Chirstopher A. Neilson - Since 2024

By working with a school network in the Chilean School Choice enrollment process, we treat a random subset of schools to transition from in-person to digital enrollment. This project aims to measure the effects of school retention due to lower enrollment costs.

School Budget Allocation and Digital Advisory Agents

Since 2024

Like in the Chilean attendance voucher system, public schools could need help declaring their information to receive public funding or properly use their resources. By training a smart agent with public spents and institutional rules, we could observe how smart sugestions could improve the school's butged use.

Schooling Deserts and Oases

Since 2021

School spatial distribution plays a key role in determining the general equilibrium of school enrollment. However, if school attributes are not equally distributed in space, socioeconomic segregation can determine the types of schools that different profiles of families have access to.
